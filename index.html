Pushing Boundaries, Redefining Possible

Danny Way is a legendary professional skateboarder renowned for pushing the boundaries of what's possible on a skateboard. Born in Portland, Oregon and raised in San Diego, California, Danny has revolutionized skateboarding through his fearless approach and innovative spirit.

He is best known for jumping the Great Wall of China in 2005, pioneering the Mega Ramp, and setting multiple world records. As a two-time Thrasher Magazine Skater of the Year and multiple X Games gold medalist, Danny's influence on skateboarding is immeasurable.
